Screen Health Checker

About the App

Discover the Screen Health Checker, an innovative tool to assess and maintain the vitality of your phone screen. Our app offers powerful tests to detect burn-ins, dead pixels, and color shades, ensuring a vibrant and clear display.

Key Features

  • Solid Color Test: Identify residuals, shades & dead pixels with vivid hues for a pristine display.
  • Static Image/Shape Residuals Test: Detect ghosting, residuals & shades effortlessly.
  • Color Gamut Test: Check color accuracy & consistency across the spectrum.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This section outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Screen Health Checker app.

Our app does not collect any personal information from you. We do not store or share any data related to your screen assessments or usage of the app.

The app only test display, and it is solely used to perform the screen assessment tests. We do not access, store, or transmit any data collected by your device.

Please note that the app may display ads from third-party ad networks. These ads are provided by the ad networks, and we do not collect any personal information through the ads. For more information about how third-party ad networks handle data, please refer to their respective privacy policies.
